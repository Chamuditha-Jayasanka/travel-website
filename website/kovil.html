Temple of Sri Kailawasanathan Swami Devasthanam Kovil

Sri Kailawasanathan Swami Devasthanam Kovil also referred to as Kapikaawatha Shivan Temple is a popular attraction and must visit places located in Colombo. This Kovil is the oldest Hindu Temple in Colombo and is devoted to Gods Ganesha and Shiva and is one of the most popular matrimonial venues. This Hindu Temple is said to have been built by a group of Indian Chetti traders known as Thiruvilanga Nakarathar.
