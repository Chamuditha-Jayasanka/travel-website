Gangaramaya (Vihara) Buddhist Temple

It is a combination of a temple and a museum. The statues of Buddhas are amazing. The museum part actually needs a guide since a self guided tour confuses a visitor on multiple counts. The entry fee is LKR 400 for a foreigner. We are supposed to leave our foot wear in a basket before entering the temple. That is a very good concept which prevents scattering of foot wear near the entrance.
