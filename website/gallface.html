Galle Face Green

If you are in Colombo definitely have to visit this place as it get really busy during evening hours. A popular place for the locals with a lot of small vendors in and around the place. Decent food served and a superb atmosphere..!! Huge hotels can be seen on the other side with luxury fine dining options but prefer this small busy place
